About

Driven by curiosity and empathy for others, I’m a product designer interested in blending distinctive design with intentional strategy.



Having graduated from Duke University with a Bachelor’s in Engineering and a minor in Visual Media Studies, I am a self-taught product designer with quick problem-solving skills and an eye for crafting engaging visual experiences.


With a passion for anything that inspires and fuels my creativity, I have since done a mix of UI/UX, graphic design, brand identity design, and animation projects. What I care most about is working alongside people who are committed to positive change.

Design Strengths

I’m a huuuuge advocate for good UX in our day-to-day work. I aim to stretch the limits of what we can achieve as a team.

“Get It Done” Mindset
