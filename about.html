Driven by curiosity and empathy for others, I’m a product designer dedicated to bridging high-fidelity craft with intentional product strategy.



I graduated from Duke University with a dual degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science and a minor in Visual Media Studies. This background naturally shaped me into a product-minded designer—I’m just as comfortable diving into technical discovery and shaping product visions as I am polishing the final details. I also love the challenge of scaling design systems to keep complex platforms consistent and human-centered.


When I’m not at my desk, I’m usually at the wheel practicing ceramics, or working on a mix of graphic design, brand identity, and animation projects. I’ve found that working across different mediums, whether digital or tactile, keeps my perspective fresh. Ultimately, I’m most at home on teams that value intentional design and are genuinely committed to making a positive impact.

Design Strengths



Product-Minded Strategy

I focus on the "why" behind every feature, leading discovery work and formulating product visions that ensure our design goals are always grounded in a clear roadmap.



UX Advocacy

I champion the user at every stage, helping the team push the boundaries of what’s possible while keeping user-centered reasoning at the heart of our decisions.



Precision and Craft

I thrive in the "last 10%" of a project, refining interactions and maintaining system-wide consistency to ensure the final product stays true to the vision.
